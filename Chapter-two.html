MY FOSTER HOME

I was moved from a home in shambles to my first foster home in Agege Lagos when I was still litte. I could remember vividly just playing around and feeling nothing.
I wasn't in school or something not even in K.G or Nursery classes at least to enhance my senses. I was just left to roam the streets of Agege.
My caregiver then was a single mom too who had kids in the village but was a courtesan (in polite measures LOL) so i was exposed to that life at a very tender age. I could see men come in and out of our
room and most time i would be inside pretending to be asleep and then one brother would come in and they'd be humping on the bed (LOL) me and my keen senses would be listening
it wasn't a pleasant idea or place or environmet to grow up in but we don't look like what we've been through (Smiles).
